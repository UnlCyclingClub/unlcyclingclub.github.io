Revamped and better than ever, the Tour De Husker has been re-imagined to provide top level courses, venues, and prizes to create an unforgettable weekend of racing.

The UNL Cycling Club introduces the 2020 Tour De Husker and it is better than ever. All events have been worked over and changed to create better racing and an exciting atmosphere. The road course and time trial are still at our beloved Branched Oak State Park. Our second day of criterium racing returns to the historic Railyard district of downtown Lincoln. Plenty of awesome sponsors will be providing prizes. We hope to see you on the starting line!
